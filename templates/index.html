INTRODUCTION

What is DeepPD all about?

Every day, lots of crimes take place everywhere around the world but the police rarely catch the culprits. Police try to get the description of the culprit from eyewitnesses.This requires the artist to have years of experience in drawing peopleâ€™s faces, Furthermore, this process is time-consuming and expensive.
DeepPD will help users to draw rough sketches and probably get realistic looking images in return that would be helpful in solving the above problem Even an inexperienced person can use this tool to get his/her imaginations out into reality.




USE CASES

Here are some of the business opportunities through DeepPD


Security Departments

By means of such people and deep learning models which can create images from free hand sketches, the police can seek help in identifying a criminal after creating sketches which would ultimately be helpful in catching those culprits and result in decline in crime rate.

3D Modeling

3D modeling is a concept of creating a 3-dimensional representation of an image. DeepPD can be used to generate image & can be further processed using 3D models helping people who are linked to film and television industry, product design, animations, game development etc.
